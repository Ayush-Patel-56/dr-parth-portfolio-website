ABOUT

Meet Dr. Parth

A dedicated neurologist committed to providing exceptional patient care through advanced diagnosis and personalized treatment

Dr. Parth is a board-certified neurologist with over 15 years of experience specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders. He completed his medical degree at a Prestigious Medical University and his neurology residency at City General Hospital.

With a focus on patient-centered care, Dr. Parth combines cutting-edge medical knowledge with compassionate treatment approaches. He believes in thoroughly explaining diagnoses and treatment options, ensuring patients feel informed and empowered throughout their healthcare journey.

Her areas of expertise include migraine management, epilepsy, movement disorders, and cognitive neurology. Dr. Parth is actively involved in clinical research and has published numerous papers in leading medical journals.
